About DovFest

The Dov Fest is the first and only music festival of it’s kind.

Located in the beautiful city of New Cuyama, CA, Dov Fest features world renowned DJs and Performers, 6 stages, themed attractions, hot air balloon rides, pop up shops, gourmet food and drink vendors, beer garderns, interactive creative art, a silent disco, stilt performers, costumed characters, private cabanas, and art installations all on the unique backgrop of a 400 acre custom built facility.
