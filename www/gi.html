General Information

TRASH IN CAMPGROUNDS
This is a GO GREEN-KEEP IT CLEAN event

DOV Fest is 100% committed to the total annihilation of the destructive carbon footprint and the nurturing of a healthier Earth for the benefit of all life sharing this beautiful planet!

The entire energy of DOV fest calls for us to work together to create the Utopia of Positive Responsibility when it comes to the management of all perishables & other forms of trash. Let's make sure we put our core beliefs into practice this weekend & here are some quick tips on how we can accomplish this TOGETHER:

A. Simply properly manage the disposal of all your own perishables or trash into one of our many nearby Recycle/Trash receptacles.

B. If you see one of your DOV Fest neighbors drop or leave any perishables/trash on any part of the festival grounds unknowingly or left irresponsibly on purpose; simply pick it up for them & dispose of it properly. Help us maintain DOV Fest & the Utopia of Positive Responsibility.

BOX OFFICE / GATE HOURS
Friday: 9am - 11pm
Saturday: 10am - 11pm
Sunday: 10am - 4pm
MONEY EXCHANGE/DOV COINS
DOV Coins are required to purchase food, beverages, camping supplies, and all other merchandise on festival grounds. "DOV Coins" will be available for purchase near all stages, food & beverage areas including everywhere specially marked on your DOV Fest map.
CAMPSITES
Campgrounds will be separately named to help you coordinate with friends and other festival attendees exactly where you will be camping. Cell reception is available on the property so finding others should not be too hard. A detailed festival map will be available on our website as well as available upon entry to the event so you will be able to coordinate with your friends and plan ahead.
CAMPING COORDINATORS (CC)
A camping coordinator will be stationed at each camp ground to advise you and to help make sure you are camping in the appropriate areas as well as answer questions that arise during your DOV Fest experience. In an event that you need security, medical or law enforcement the camping coordinator located on your camp ground with be available to advise and assist you.
PARK-AND-SLEEP CAMPING PACKAGES
Dov Fest will be offering all inclusive “Park-and-sleep” camping packages for a convenient and stress-free experience. For the full list of packages available please visit the tickets page here: squadup.com/dovfest
SHOWERS
Showers will be easily available on the festival campgrounds for a nominal fee. Make sure you bring your toiletries and your favorite towel.
FIRE PITS
Fire pits will be located throughout DOV Fest on festival grounds as well and camp grounds. If you don’t have a designated fire pit in your area, then it means that absolutely no fires are allowed in that area. If you are unsure about whether you can have a fire please ask your CC before starting one.
GRILLING & COOKING
Propane powered grills are allowed in the camping areas, however absolutely NO open flame or charcoal grills are permitted.
CAMPING PASSES
3 Day & One day DOV Fest passes include free tent camping in the festival. This means that you will park your car in the GA parking lot and walk or take the DOV Fest shuttle to get to the campgrounds. GA camping areas fill up on a first come first serve basis so make sure you get there early. Saving spots for friends is not permitted so make sure your group shows up together.
MOBILE CAMPING (CAR / RV CAMPING)
Purchasing a mobile camping ticket allows you to bring your car/RV onto campgrounds and have it next to you while you camp. There will be a separate designated are for people with MOBILE CAMPING passes.

**NOTE: there are NO RV hookups stations located at the venue so if your coming in an RV make sure you plan accordingly
SAFETY AND SECURITY AGE
This is an 18+ event. You will be carded at the festival gates so make sure you bring your ID. Designated beer gardens located on festival grounds require a 21+ wristband. No Exceptions.
SECURITY/FIRST AID/EMTS
DOV Fest security and medical teams will be on patrol and stationed 24 hours a day throughout the festival in case of an emergency. In the event that you need assistance, you can approach any DOV Fest staff member, information area, or first aid area.
DRUGS & ILLEGAL SUBSTANCES
DOV Fest is a drug free event. Anyone caught with or doing any illegal substances will be escorted from the event and may face legal consequences. In addition to the Dov Fest Security team on site New Cuyama Sheriff’s will be assisting us throughout the festival in the event that they are needed.
NUDITY
Public nudity is not allowed at DOV Fest. You might be in the middle of nowhere… but that doesn’t mean, NO WEAR.
FIRE
Open fires are only permitted in designated fire pits located on campgrounds.
