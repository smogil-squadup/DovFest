Partnerships and Efforts

DOV Fest partners with SURF RIDER to clean up the Santa Barbara oil spills

Dov Fest is located within the ocean-side southern Californian county of Santa Barbara, home to beautiful beaches and stunning country sides.

This area also unfortunately experiences consistant oil spills from on-shore and off-shore oil pipelines. Over the past 7 years alone, Santa Barbara has suffered more than 170 oil spills including the major spill that occurred this May by Refugio State Beach.

Dov Fest is teaming up with such charities as Surf Rider and Clean Seas to help raise awareness of this reoccurring issue and contribute to clean up efforts. Dov Fest will be raising money to aid these organizations by donating $4 from every ticket to help them keep our oceans and beaches clean.
